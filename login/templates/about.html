Who We Are?

LearnPy is an online learning platform that offers a user-friendly interface and a comprehensive range of courses and activities. The platform provides a seamless learning experience for users of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners.

How to use LearnPy?

  1. Sign up and log in
  2. Choose if you want to browse for courses or activities
  3. Click the start button and review the details of your chosen course/activity
  4. Click "Learn" to start the course/activity
